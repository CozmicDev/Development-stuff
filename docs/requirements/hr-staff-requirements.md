# HR Staff Requirements

## 1. Employee Lifecycle Management

### 1.1 Recruitment and Hiring
- Post job openings
- Track applicants and candidates
- Schedule interviews
- Manage offer letters
- Conduct background checks
- Track hiring pipeline and metrics
- Manage recruitment agencies
- Collaborate with hiring managers

### 1.2 Onboarding
- Create onboarding workflows
- Assign onboarding tasks and checklist
- Track new hire documentation
- Verify employment eligibility (I-9, work permits, etc.)
- Set up new employee accounts
- Manage probation periods
- Track onboarding completion
- Collect new hire feedback

### 1.3 Employee Records Management
- Create and maintain employee profiles
- Upload and manage employee documents
- Track employment history
- Manage contract renewals
- Update employee information
- Track certifications and licenses
- Manage dependent information
- Archive terminated employee records

### 1.4 Transfers and Promotions
- Process internal transfers
- Manage promotions
- Update job titles and grades
- Adjust compensation
- Track organizational changes
- Generate transfer/promotion letters
- Update reporting structures

### 1.5 Offboarding
- Process resignation/termination
- Manage exit interviews
- Create exit checklists
- Generate clearance certificates
- Process final settlements
- Recover company assets
- Conduct exit surveys
- Manage rehire eligibility
